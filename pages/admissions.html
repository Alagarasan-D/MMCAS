Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common admission queries

The admission process involves: 1) Online application submission, 2) Entrance examination (if applicable), 3) Merit list announcement, 4) Counseling session, 5) Document verification, 6) Fee payment, and 7) Final admission confirmation. The entire process is designed to be transparent and student-friendly.

For UG programs, candidates must have completed 12th standard with minimum 50% marks (45% for reserved categories). Specific programs may have additional subject requirements. For example, B.Sc Computer Science requires Mathematics in 12th standard.

Yes, MMCAS conducts an entrance examination for most programs. The exam tests aptitude, general knowledge, and subject-specific knowledge. However, some programs may offer direct admission based on 12th standard marks. Check the specific program requirements for details.

Required documents include: 10th and 12th mark sheets, transfer certificate, migration certificate (if applicable), Aadhaar card, passport size photographs, character certificate, medical fitness certificate, and category certificate (if applicable). All documents should be self-attested.

Yes, MMCAS offers various scholarships including merit-based scholarships, need-based financial aid, sports scholarships, and government scholarships. Students from economically weaker sections can avail up to 100% fee waiver. Apply early to maximize scholarship opportunities.

Yes, the college offers flexible payment options. Annual fees can be paid in two or three installments. However, at least 50% of the total fee must be paid at the time of admission. Contact the accounts department for customized payment plans.
